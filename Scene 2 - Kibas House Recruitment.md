# Scene 2 - Kibas House Recruitment

**Setting:** Interior, Kiba's Living Room - Early Morning / Day
**Characters:** Shikamaru Nara, Kiba Inuzuka, Lizzy (Kiba's wife), Akamaru (Giant Ninken)

### Shot 1
* **Camera:** Wide Shot (High Angle)
* **Visual:** The camera shows a messy, dimly lit living room. A huge dog (Akamaru) is resting on a central rug. Kiba, wearing a hood, is leaning lazily against the dog with a beer bottle in hand, staring blankly at the floor. Nearby, Lizzy sits on the sofa, knitting wool. Shikamaru stands ominously in the doorway.

### Shot 2
* **Camera:** Medium Shot (Over Shikamaru's Shoulder)
* **Visual:** Lizzy stops knitting and looks up at Shikamaru, her expression a mix of surprise and annoyance.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "Lord Hokage, what brings you to our home?"
* **Audio / Dialogue (Shikamaru):** "I have urgent matters I need your husband for."

### Shot 3
* **Camera:** Close Up (On Lizzy)
* **Visual:** Lizzy glares toward Kiba on the floor.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "My husband? What good is a drunk to you?"

### Shot 4
* **Camera:** Low Angle Medium Shot (On Kiba)
* **Visual:** The camera is positioned low to the ground. Kiba shifts slightly against Akamaru, glancing over lazily at Shikamaru. 
* **Audio / Dialogue (Shikamaru):** "There isn't a more competent ninja in the village at your husband's specialty."
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "If getting drunk at these early hours of the morning was a skill, then I must agree, Lord Hokage."
* **Audio / Dialogue (Kiba):** "Shut up, woman."

### Shot 5
* **Camera:** Medium Close Up (On Kiba)
* **Visual:** Kiba locks eyes with Shikamaru. He looks tired and completely unbothered by the Hokage's presence.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Kiba):** "Hey, Shikamaru, didn't you get my letter? I'm retired. I have no business with the Hokage."

### Shot 6
* **Camera:** Over the Shoulder (From Kiba's Perspective)
* **Visual:** Shikamaru looks down at Kiba. He is dead serious, refusing to take no for an answer.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Shikamaru):** "I will have a team of Chunin ready to go. If they are to be successful, they will need your guidance."
* **Visual Action:** Without waiting for an argument, Shikamaru turns and leaves through the door.
