# Scene 3 - Kiba and Lizzy Argument

**Setting:** Interior, Kiba's Dining Room - Midday
**Characters:** Kiba Inuzuka, Lizzy, Akamaru

### Shot 1
* **Camera:** Profile Wide Shot
* **Visual:** The camera captures the dining table from the side. Lizzy is placing food down onto plates. A large dog bowl sits on the floor nearby where Akamaru is eating. Kiba drags himself to the table and collapses heavily into his chair across from Lizzy.

### Shot 2
* **Camera:** Over the Shoulder (Looking at Kiba)
* **Visual:** Looking past Lizzy's shoulder as she stares at Kiba. Kiba looks exhausted and miserable.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "I think you should go. The village needs you—that’s why the Hokage came personally."
* **Audio / Dialogue (Kiba):** "Tough luck. I'm a wine seller now. If the village isn't looking to buy wine, then I have nothing to offer."

### Shot 3
* **Camera:** Over the Shoulder (Looking at Lizzy)
* **Visual:** Looking past Kiba at Lizzy. She shakes her head, visibly disappointed.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "Wine seller, my foot. You drink more wine than you sell."

### Shot 4
* **Camera:** Medium Shot (On Kiba)
* **Visual:** Kiba grits his teeth, looking extremely annoyed.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Kiba):** "I just want to eat in peace. Will you stop being a drag?"
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "I'm sure the Sixth and Seventh Hokage would think..."

### Shot 5
* **Camera:** Close Up (On Kiba)
* **Visual:** Kiba snaps. He slams his hand on the table, furious at the mention of Naruto.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Kiba):** "The Seventh Hokage wouldn't stand with his tail between his legs while the Divine Trees are running riot in the village, would he now?!"

### Shot 6
* **Camera:** Medium Shot (On Lizzy)
* **Visual:** Lizzy stares at him, her expression hardening. She stands up from her chair.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Lizzy):** "I got married to the bravest and most optimistic ninja in the village—not the strongest. I don't even know what I'm looking at right now." 
* **Visual Action:** She turns and walks out of the room.

### Shot 7
* **Camera:** Low Angle (Looking at the Wall)
* **Visual:** The camera watches Kiba glare toward the door. In a fit of rage, he grabs his bottle and hurls it hard against the back wall. It shatters dramatically.
* **Audio / Dialogue (Kiba):** "Shit!"
