# Scene 1 - Hokages Office

**Setting:** Interior, Hokage's Office - Night
**Characters:** Shikamaru Nara

### Shot 1
* **Camera:** Wide Establishing Shot (2.5D Billboard Zoom)
* **Visual:** A 2D matte painting of the Hidden Leaf Village at night projected onto a flat billboard. The camera slowly pushes in (zooms) toward the Hokage building. Just as we get close to the lit office window, we cut.

### Shot 2
* **Camera:** Outside Window (Looking In)
* **Visual:** The camera looks through the massive circular window behind the Hokage. We see the back of Shikamaru's chair, with the office and door visible in the background.

### Shot 3
* **Camera:** Wide Front View (From the Door)
* **Visual:** Cut to inside the office. We see a wide shot of Shikamaru sitting at his desk, buried under massive stacks of paperwork, with the window illuminating him from behind. He rubs his temples, stressed from the Feudal Lord's pressure.
* **Audio / V.O. (Shikamaru):** "Bug... one of the Divine Tree clones is preparing to attack. All of our top Jonin will be completely occupied."

### Shot 4
* **Camera:** Close Up (On Shikamaru)
* **Visual:** The camera cuts close to Shikamaru's face as his eyes dart across the anonymous tip about the rogue Hyuga. He leans back heavily in his chair, frustrated by the timing.
* **Audio / V.O. (Shikamaru):** "...And now this. A rogue Hyuga clan member acting as an assassin. Capturing a Byakugan user requires a tracking specialist... I need the best tracker we have left."
* **Transition:** Hard cut to the dim, messy interior of Kiba's house.
